Paperless Session – Support

Welcome to the support page for the Paperless Meetings app. This app allows you to view, save, and manage your official documents and resolutions directly from your website.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I use the app?
A: Simply open the app and it will load your website. Tap any PDF or link to view and save documents.

Q: Can I use the app offline?
A: Yes, documents that you've saved are available for offline viewing in the app.

Q: Where are my documents stored?
A: All files are stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded or shared externally.

Q: Can I use my own website?
A: Yes! You can configure the app to load your own official website and download PDF files directly from it.
